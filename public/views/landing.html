How ACEAS works

From registration to approval in four simple steps.

1
Register Your Project

Describe your AI system — type, purpose, dataset, and use case — to create a compliance project.

2
Answer 20 Questions

Complete the structured ethics questionnaire covering all five compliance domains with simple Yes/No answers.

3
Get Your Score & AI Report

Receive an instant compliance score, risk classification, and AI-generated improvement recommendations.

4
Officer Review & Approval

A compliance officer reviews your submission and provides a final decision with feedback.
